Kia Ora! Welcome to The Haukai Restaurant

Welcome to The Haukai Restaurant. Nau mai, haere mai! Haukai is the Maori word for a feast and we believe that good kai has the power to make people whanau. Like Aotearoa, we are a young place, but our offerings are steeped in rich traditions and flavours. At Haukai, we are devoted to the exploration of food which is linked to our whenua and the iwis of our land. Haukai is driven to passing down the traditions of our Maori ancestors through the dishes we present. We see it as our mission to inati or share the tastes which are a part of an identity which can be traced to our earliest ancestors from Haiwaiki. Haukai is the realisation of a dream to invigorate the dining experience with a good old taste of Kiwi and the bonds which forged our nation. Food has the power to fill our bodies and to fill our minds when we come together to savour it. As a Maori proverb says “he kai kei aku ringa”, there is food at the end of my hands. Living a meaningful life means having good food to get us there. Join us in this wonderful journey of living!
